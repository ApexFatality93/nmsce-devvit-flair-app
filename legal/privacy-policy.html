NMSCE Flair App Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the NMSCE Flair App ("the app") handles information when installed in a subreddit on Reddit.

What the App Does

The app helps moderators validate, repair, and manage post flairs in subreddits where the app is installed. It can also respond to certain moderator comment commands and optionally send moderation logs to a Discord webhook configured by subreddit moderators.

Information the App Processes

The app may process the following Reddit content and metadata:

  • post titles, post bodies, post IDs, subreddit names, and post flair data
  • comment bodies, comment IDs, and comment author usernames
  • post author usernames
  • subreddit configuration entered by moderators into the app settings

How the App Uses Information

  • to validate whether a post flair contains required information
  • to repair or normalize flair text when enough information is available
  • to remove or restore posts according to moderator configuration and subreddit rules
  • to post automated moderator-assistance comments
  • to detect moderator-only comment commands such as !redirect and !request
  • to optionally send moderation and flair action logs to a Discord webhook configured by moderators

Temporary Storage

The app may temporarily store limited operational data using Reddit/Devvit storage, including prompt-state markers and recently seen or repaired flair values. This storage is used only to support app behavior such as preventing duplicate prompt comments and improving flair repair behavior.

Discord Webhook Logging

If subreddit moderators configure a Discord webhook URL, the app may send event logs to that Discord destination. These logs can include post titles, flair changes, Reddit links, post authors, moderator usernames, and other moderation context relevant to the action taken.

Discord webhook logging is optional and is controlled by subreddit moderators.

Data Sharing

The app does not sell personal information. Information is only processed as needed to operate within Reddit and, if enabled by moderators, to send webhook logs to Discord.

Data Retention

Operational data is retained only as long as reasonably necessary for the app to function. Some temporary values may expire automatically.

Children's Privacy

The app is intended for use within Reddit communities and is not directed specifically to children.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The current version should be kept at the same public URL used in the app settings.
